About SJ Contracts

SJ Contracts is a leading construction company in Pune, India with over three decades of comprehensive experience in the industry. Since 1990, we have been providing solution-based construction services that add value to industrial, commercial and residential construction projects.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery has made us a trusted partner for numerous clients across various sectors. We combine innovative construction techniques with traditional values to deliver exceptional results.
