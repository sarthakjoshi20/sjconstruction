Current Job Openings

Senior Project Manager

Full-time Pune 8+ years experience

We are looking for an experienced Senior Project Manager to oversee large-scale construction projects from conception to completion. The ideal candidate will have strong leadership skills and extensive experience in construction management.

Responsibilities:
  • Manage project timelines, budgets, and resources
  • Coordinate with clients, architects, and subcontractors
  • Ensure compliance with safety regulations and quality standards
  • Lead project teams and provide technical guidance
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or related field
  • 8+ years of construction project management experience
  • PMP certification preferred
  • Strong knowledge of construction processes and regulations

Site Engineer

Full-time Mumbai 3+ years experience

We are seeking a dedicated Site Engineer to oversee construction activities, ensure quality control, and provide technical support on our project sites.

Responsibilities:
  • Supervise construction activities and ensure compliance with designs
  • Conduct quality checks and maintain documentation
  • Coordinate with contractors and resolve technical issues
  • Monitor project progress and report to senior management
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
  • 3+ years of site engineering experience
  • Strong technical knowledge and problem-solving skills
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD and MS Office

Safety Officer

Full-time Pune 2+ years experience

We are looking for a Safety Officer to ensure compliance with occupational health and safety guidelines and promote a culture of safety across all our construction sites.

Responsibilities:
  • Implement and monitor safety protocols on construction sites
  • Conduct safety inspections and risk assessments
  • Organize safety training programs for employees
  • Investigate accidents and implement corrective measures
Requirements:
  • Diploma/Degree in Industrial Safety or related field
  • 2+ years of experience in construction safety
  • Knowledge of local safety regulations and standards
  • Strong communication and training skills
