About Us

Our Core Purpose

We exist to make our clients’ lives easier by providing excellent and value-added construction services. To ensure this, all other stakeholder relationships are fulfilled with the same purpose in mind.

Our Values

Our values represent what we stand for as a corporate citizen, a business partner and an employer. They inspire us and will be embodied by our employees at every level, function of our business. Our prevailing company values are

I bring a 100% effort into everything that I undertake. I have the desire to achieve my personal and company goals.

I am willing and able to improve myself on a constant basis. I respond to challenges by being open-minded.

Respecting the individual is fundamental to all my interactions. I always focus on the problem and not on the person.

I put my mind as well as my heart into work. I build enjoyment into daily activities. I like to work in an energetic and rewarding environment.

Our Vision

The idea of seeing SJCPL as one of the most sought after contractor is what excites us the most. SJCPL shall be a professionally run organisation. The leadership will comprise of substantial individuals able to drive further growth in alignment to the company’s values and culture. We will have created a respectable standing in the market and will be a much sought after contractor for private as well as public sector projects. The quality of our services and the technology used will be identified as the standard benchmark for the construction industry. Employee welfare and happiness will still be a key priority. We will be the most preferred employer in the construction sector. Our employee experience will be such that all employees will feel a sense of familial attachment to the company.

Our Team

The strength of our team is exhibited in the shared passion we have for building and the respect we show towards our colleagues.

Leadership

SJ Contracts has benefited from energetic, principled, and creative leaders at its helm.

Suhas Jangle

Chairman

BE Civil, IIT Madras

As the founder and promoter of SJ Contracts, Suhas Jangle brings to the table a formidable thirty-five years of experience in the construction industry. Prior to starting up SJ Contracts he has worked with renowned construction companies on large-scale industrial projects across India, the Middle East and Africa. Currently he assumes the role of principal and chief mentor driving overall management and business development of the company.

Saurabh Jangle

Executive Director

Masters in Construction Management – Texas A&M University, USAbr

Saurabh Jangle joined as Director in the year 2006. He has since then spearheaded execution and project management of all SJCPL projects. He currently also takes the executive lead on all tendering processes. Saurabh is responsible for introducing the technological advancements in the construction practices adopted by at SJCPL. Prior to joining SJCPL, he has worked with Jacobs Engineering on EPC projects in the USA.

Bhargav Jangle

Executive Director

Masters in Management – EDHEC Business School, France

Bhargav Jangle is currently managing the administration and support functions at SJCPL. Bhargav brings diversity to the collective senior management experience at SJCPL. Prior to SJCPL, he has worked in the banking and pharmaceutical sectors in compliances and strategic procurement respectively.
